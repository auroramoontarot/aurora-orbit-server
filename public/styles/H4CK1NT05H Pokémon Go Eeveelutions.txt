H4CK1NT05H: Pokémon Go, you can guarantee a specific Eevee evolution by renaming it before evolving (costs 25 Eevee candy). This trick works only once per evolution per account. Key names include Kira (Sylveon), Rainer (Vaporeon), Sparky (Jolteon), Pyro (Flareon), Sakura (Espeon), Tamao (Umbreon), Linnea (Leafeon), and Rea (Glaceon).
